## Scope
This work documents publicly observable activity on Telegram.

Included:
- public channels and groups
- public user profiles
- publicly shared invitation links
- publicly visible messages and forwards

Excluded:
- private messages
- secret chats
- social engineering

### Activity Involving Third Parties
When observed activities involve third-party harm
(e.g. stolen data, financial instruments, identity documents):

- no interaction is performed
- no baiting is conducted
- observation and reporting are the only actions taken

Scam baiting is limited to cases defined in:
- [Actions Following Observation](actions.md#scam-baiting-boundaries)
