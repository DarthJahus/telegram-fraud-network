## Reports
Reports document external actions taken in response to observed activity.

This may include:
- IC3 reports (date, report ID)
- Telegram abuse reports (email-based)
- registrar or hosting provider reports

Each report links to:
- related incidents
- involved Telegram accounts
- supporting evidence or email chains (`.eml` if available)

Reports document action, not enforcement or platform response.

---

Reports are created in response to documented harm.

See:
- [Incidents](incidents.md)
